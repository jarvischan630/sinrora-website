FQA | Getting Started

I only have an idea. Can I still start my beauty brand?

Absolutely. Many clients start with only an idea, target market, or product concept. We can support product selection, fragrance direction, packaging, and customization planning.

What products can I customize with JUVYGLOW?

We provide OEM/ODM services for skincare, makeup, perfumes, body care products, and selected beauty accessories, supporting both single products and complete beauty lines.

Can I customize formula, packaging, and logo?

Yes. We support product development, formula customization, packaging design, logo printing, private label solutions, and brand-oriented development.

What is your MOQ requirement?

MOQ depends on the product category, packaging type, and customization level. We also discuss suitable solutions for startup brands and growing businesses.

I am a distributor / wholesaler. Can I build my own brand?

Absolutely. We work with startups, beauty brands, distributors, wholesalers, and retail businesses looking to develop private label beauty products.
